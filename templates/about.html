About

Welcome to the OpenCV Image Filters Web App! This application is designed to provide users with an intuitive interface to apply various image processing filters using the powerful OpenCV library. Our app supports a range of filters, including Sobel and Scharr, allowing users to explore the capabilities of these image processing techniques in real-time. The primary objective of this application is to demonstrate the use of OpenCV filters in a simple and accessible manner. Whether you're an enthusiast looking to learn more about image processing or a developer seeking to experiment with different filters, our web app offers a user-friendly platform to achieve your goals.
